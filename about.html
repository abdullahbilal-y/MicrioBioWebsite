About Me

Welcome to my professional portfolio. I'm Zafran Ullah Khattak, a dedicated microbiologist with expertise in food safety, quality control, and laboratory diagnostics.

With an M.Phil in Microbiology and additional qualifications in pharmacy and safety management, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to my work in microbiology and public health.

"My research focuses on the intersection of microbiology, food safety, and public health, with a particular interest in developing innovative approaches to combat foodborne pathogens."

Throughout my career, I have worked across various sectors including food processing facilities, diagnostic laboratories, and academic research institutions. This diverse experience has equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of microbial ecology, pathogen detection techniques, and quality management systems.

I am passionate about applying microbiological principles to solve real-world problems and improve public health outcomes. Through rigorous scientific inquiry and practical application, I strive to contribute meaningfully to the field of microbiology.
